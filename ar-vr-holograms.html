AR, VR & Holograms

Augmented Reality (AR)

Augmented Reality overlays digital content such as images, videos, or text onto the real world. It enhances reality without replacing it.

Virtual Reality (VR)

Virtual Reality creates a fully immersive digital environment using VR headsets. It is used in gaming, training, and simulations.

Holograms

Holograms are three-dimensional images created using light technology. They appear to float and can be viewed from different angles.
